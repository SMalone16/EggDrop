Focus Questions

Use the question spotlight to slow down and think deeply. Discuss one prompt at a time with your team, then capture your ideas on sticky notes or in your notebook.

How can we design a device that keeps our egg safe during a drop? What forces will push or pull on our egg protector? Which materials at school or home could cushion the egg the best? How will gravity and air resistance work together during the fall? Where do we predict the egg might crack first and why? How can we test ideas safely before using a real egg? What designs from nature inspire a protective shell? What questions should we ask our classmates or teachers to learn more? How will we know if our design is ready for the official launch? What changes might we try after the first test to make it even better? How will we explain our design choices to another engineering team? What do we predict will happen in our post-test drop after making improvements?
