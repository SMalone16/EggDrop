Welcome to the Egg Drop Mission

You are the engineer in charge. Your team must invent a device that protects a raw egg during a drop. Use each phase of the engineering design process to ask bold questions, explore ideas, model prototypes, evaluate results, and explain discoveries.

How to Use This Guide

  • Start with the Ask phase to spark your curiosity.
  • Gather background knowledge and inspiration in Explore.
  • Sketch, build, and refine in Model.
  • Test like scientists in Evaluate.
  • Share your learning story in Explain.

Design Promises

Engineers work with empathy, creativity, and evidence. As you design, remember to:

  • Respect teammates and listen deeply.
  • Record every idea and test result.
  • Iterate quickly and celebrate small wins.

Mission Timeline

Plan your studio time with intentional checkpoints.

  1. Wonder & define the challenge.
  2. Research forces, materials, and inspiring designs.
  3. Sketch prototypes and build a first model.
  4. Run a sequence of chair-height drop tests.
  5. Capture results, revise, and prepare to explain.
Tip: Keep the design wheel open on your tablet during work sessions so you always know the next move.
