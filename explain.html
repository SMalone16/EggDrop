Communicate Like an Engineer

Use media, data, and reflection to show how your team solved the Egg Drop Challenge.

Video Story Prompts

  • Introduce your team and show the final model close up.
  • Explain how each part protects the egg using science words.
  • Show a clip of your best test and describe what the data means.
  • Share how you improved the design between trials.

iPad Recording Tips

  • Use landscape mode and steady hands or a tripod.
  • Check audio levels and speak clearly toward the microphone.
  • Enable captions or add text labels for key vocabulary.
  • Record in a quiet space to reduce background noise.

Reflect & Celebrate

Reflection Questions

  • What surprised you during testing?
  • How did your team share responsibilities?
  • What would you change for a higher drop?
