Test Protocol

Follow this plan so every team collects reliable data.

  1. Inspect your model for loose parts before testing.
  2. Load a plastic practice egg filled with pebbles to mimic the weight of a real egg.
  3. Hold the device at chair height (about 45 cm) and keep arms straight out.
  4. Count down 3-2-1 together before dropping for consistent timing.
  5. Observe the landing area before touching the model and describe what you see.
Switch to a real egg only after two successful practice drops.

Safety & Team Roles

Safety Reminders

  • Wear safety glasses during all drop tests.
  • Keep the landing zone clear of hands, faces, and backpacks.
  • Use a broom and dustpan immediately if an egg breaks.
  • Wash hands after handling raw eggs.

Engineer Roles

  • Drop Technician: releases the device smoothly from the set height.
  • Data Recorder: times drops, fills in the table, and notes observations.
  • Safety Captain: checks glasses, clears the landing zone, and leads cleanup.
  • Design Analyst: suggests quick adjustments between trials.
